Hey, all👋

+ I am LYU Chengshang, a PhD student majoring in Bioinformatics and Computational Biology at City University of Hong Kong.

+ I am a member of [CompbioClub](https://compbio.club) directed by [Prof. CHEN Lingxi](https://thelingxichen.github.io/).

+ I received my B.Eng. in Information Engineering from Xi'an Jiaotong University and M.Eng. in Bioinformatics from CAS Center for Excellence in Molecular Cell Science supervised by [Prof. LIU Xiaoping](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~liuxp) and [P.I. CHEN Luonan](https://people.ucas.ac.cn/~0012240).

+ My research interest mainly includes Dynamic Network Biomarker (DNB), Sample Specific Network (SSN), Gene Regulatory Network (GRN), information entropy and causal inference.

+ Also, I am a open-source enthusiast and there are some useful projects in my [GitHub](https://github.com/lllvcs).
